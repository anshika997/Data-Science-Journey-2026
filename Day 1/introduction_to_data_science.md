# Introduction to Data Science

## What is Data Science?
Data Science is a field that uses mathematics, statistics, programming, and domain knowledge to extract meaningful insights from data and solve real-world problems.

In simple terms:

> **Data Science is the process of collecting, cleaning, analyzing, and interpreting data to make informed decisions.**
